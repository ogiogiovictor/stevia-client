Software Engineer, Frontend - UI/UX

Posted 4 days ago

OneFi Nigeria

Full Time

Lagos, Nigeria

Expiry date

30th July, 2019

Share this job:

Description

OneFi is the fastest growing location based Virtual Reality management and content distribution platform outside of China, and we are looking for a user experience designer and researcher.

This position requires strong UX research and UX design skills that can be applied to web, mobile, and VR interfaces. You will need to leverage methodologies to study user behaviour on our platforms in order to create user-centred design solutions.

Responsibility

Developing new user experiences using React

Building reusable components and frontend libraries

Translate designs, wireframes, and business requirements into high quality UI

Requirements

A computer science qualification BA/BSc/HND

3+ years of professional experience working on digital products

Applied experience with UX research techniques, planning and running user tests, analyzing findings and articulating results.

Perks

Free Macbook

Quarterly bonus

Salary

Salary & Benefits: 100,000.00 USD / Annually
